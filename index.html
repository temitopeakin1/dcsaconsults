    About Consultant

    Dr. Stephenie .C. Alaribe (MIPAN, MRSC) is a highly motivated and enthusiastic Bio and Pharmaceutical Chemist with interest in Medicinal and Natural Product Chemistry. A member of the Institute of Public analyst of Nigeria, Dr Alaribe is bringing her experience in Natural Product Chemistry, Quality assessment of drugs and related products into the World of Drug and herbal Assessment. She is currently involved in developing Quality Control Assessment tools (QCAT) and markers in accessing Nigerian herbal and Medicinal products. Dr. Alaribe has consulted for some Health Parastatals and has also investigated some Nigerian Herbal Products which has added value to the quality of the products. She is a member of several academic groups and institutions including Society for Medicinal Plant Research (GA), Germany, Nigerian Society of Pharmacognosy, American Society of Pharmacognosy (ASP), International Society for Infectious Diseases (ISID), Societa Italo-Latin American of Ethnomedicine (SILAE, African Representative) and Organization for Women in Science for the Developing World (OWSD). Dr. Stephanie .C. Alaribe has also published and presented papers in local and international journals and has mentored and supervised over 20 undergraduates and 10 Post graduate Students including PhD students in her research group.
